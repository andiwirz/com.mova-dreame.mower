MOVA & Dreame Mower brings your robotic lawn mower into Homey. Start, pause, stop, and return to dock directly from the device card. Choose a zone or spot from the dynamic pickers and tap Start Mowing — or trigger everything from flows. Monitor battery, charging status, consumable life, and error state in real time. Both MOVA and Dreame robots are supported across European, Chinese, and North American cloud regions.

Configure cutting height, mow efficiency, edge mowing behaviour, obstacle avoidance, frost and rain protection, lighting, Do Not Disturb, Low Speed at Night, battery thresholds, child lock, volume, voice announcements, and anti-theft alarm directly in Homey. Settings are read from the mower on startup and written back immediately when changed. Full flow card support includes triggers for mowing started, mowing completed, battery low, consumable replacement, errors, and AI obstacle detection, plus actions to set cutting height and efficiency mode.
