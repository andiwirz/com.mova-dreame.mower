MOVA & Dreame Mower integrates your robotic lawn mower into Homey, giving you direct control over mowing, edge mowing, and zone management — all from a single app. Both MOVA and Dreame robots are supported across European, Chinese, and North American cloud regions.

Monitor battery level and charging status in real time. Use action buttons on the device card to start mowing, mow the full area, pause, stop, or return to the dock. Per-zone and per-zone edge mowing buttons appear automatically based on the zones detected from your map. Get notified when mowing completes, an error occurs, or a firmware update is available.

Configure a wide range of device settings directly in Homey — values are read from the mower on startup and written back immediately when you change them:

• Frost protection and rain protection (sensitivity and wait time)
• Lighting — custom LED activation time window and per-scenario behaviour (standby, mowing, charging, error)
• Do Not Disturb — quiet window during which the mower will not start automatically
• Low Speed at Night — time window during which the mower slows down to protect animals
• Battery — return-to-dock threshold, task-resume threshold and auto-resume after charging
• Child lock — lock and unlock the physical buttons on the mower
• Volume — set the speaker volume (0–100)
• Voice announcements — configure which status types the mower announces (notifications, work status, special status, errors)
• Anti-theft alarm — lift alarm, map-leave alarm and real-time location (Link module required for map and location)
• AI obstacle photos — enable photo capture of AI-detected obstacles
• Consumable status — blade life, cleaning brush life and robot maintenance remaining (%)

Full flow card support lets you automate mowing based on time, weather, or any other Homey trigger.
